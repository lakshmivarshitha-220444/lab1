Tram Services

Tram services offer an eco-friendly and efficient mode of urban transportation, ideal for short to medium distances within cities.

They help reduce traffic congestion and provide a comfortable ride for daily commuters.

pros:

  • Environmentally friendly
  • Frequent stops for easy access
  • Affordable fares

cons:

  • Slower than other modes of transport
  • Limited routes compared to buses or trains
